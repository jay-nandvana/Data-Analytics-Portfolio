# Power BI Projects

## 1. HR Analytics Dashboard
Analyzed 1,470 employees to find attrition patterns.
- Attrition rate: 16.1%
- Lab Technicians had highest exit rate
- Low salary is biggest reason for leaving

## 2. Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard
Analyzed ecommerce sales data.
- Total sales: 438K with 37K profit
- Clothing top category at 47%
- COD most used payment at 39%

## 3. Super Store Sales Dashboard
Analyzed 2 years retail sales data.
- Total sales: 1.6M with 175K profit
- West region highest at 33%
- California top revenue state
